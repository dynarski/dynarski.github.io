Restructuring Student Loans Conference

While co-director of the Education Policy Initiative, Susan Dynarski convened an international comparative conference to give policymakers, analysts, and press cross-national perspectives on student debt and repayment. The goal was to enrich the US conversation with evidence from countries that structure aid, borrowing, and repayment differently.

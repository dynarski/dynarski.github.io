Research

Download CV (PDF)

Dynarski's research examines how education policies shape opportunity, mobility, and inequality. Her work focuses on college access, financial aid, student debt, and the design of policies that help students move through school and into the labor market. She also studies K-12 reforms, charter schools, early childhood investments, and methods for using administrative data to measure educational outcomes.

Bio

Susan Dynarski is the Patricia Albjerg Graham Professor at Harvard University. Her research examines how education policies shape opportunity, mobility, and inequality, with a focus on college access, financial aid, student debt, and the design of programs that help students move through school and into the labor market.

Her work combines large administrative datasets, quasi-experimental designs, randomized trials, and policy analysis. She also writes for broader audiences about education, inequality, and evidence-based policy.

Short press bio

Susan Dynarski is the Patricia Albjerg Graham Professor at Harvard University. She studies education policy, inequality, college access, financial aid, and student debt. Her work uses rigorous evidence to inform decisions by students, families, institutions, and policymakers.
